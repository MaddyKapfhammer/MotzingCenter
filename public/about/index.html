Carrying on the legacy of Jack and Grace Motzing

In March of 1971, Jack and Grace Motzing purchased the Iris Theatre from W.L. Dunn with a mission to focus on ministry. More than 5,000 people became Christians over the 43 years that the Motzings owned the theatre. In November of 2013, Jack and Grace sold the Iris Theatre to Cochranton Community Church. In November 2016, the Motzing Center was revealed, housing the Iris Theatre and Kovfino the Coffe Lounge. The Motzing Center was built as a community ministry, and is run as a not for profit organization.
